Meetings

Carlton Hill, Quaker Meeting House, Leeds

By tradition meetings are held on the second Wednesday of each month; the theme is normally a lecture or presentation on a particular astronomical topic given by guest speakers or society members.

Meetings are of an informal nature, where members have the opportunity to show something about their interests (slides, videos, instruments, observations, etc.) occur two weeks later, also on a Wednesday.

Doors open at 7.00pm and meetings end between 9.30 and 10pm. See the map to the right to see meeting point.

Formal Meetings 2018

The Leeds Astronomical Society have planned various meetings throughout the calender year, and have some special speakers and guests appearing for the first time.

The calendar on the right has documented each of these meetings, feel free to add these events into your calendar to make sure you don't miss out!

Simply press the plus button and these will go straight into your own Google calendar which can be viewed on your phone and computer.
